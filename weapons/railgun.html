No one uses the drill. Though if you may know, it's unlocked from the terminal for 100k points and serves more as a healing device for people with skill issues than anything else. It fires a non hitscan drill that latches onto enemies and starts dealing small continuous damage to the target over 7.5 seconds, giving out large amounts of blood. You can also punch the drill while it's in an enemy to instantly deal with the remaining damage. Additionally, if your aim is good enough, you can punch it into anothe ennemy resetting the timer and the damage starts over again. If the enemy you fired the drill on has so little health that it is one shot by the initial damage from contact, the drill will just pierce it, killing it in the process and can attach itself to any enemy behind it. The long cooldown for the railcannons and the very limited tech of the drill makes it useless to players with higher skill and serves more as a crutch for newer players attempting difficult bossfights, namely minos and Sisyphus prime. The drill is, however, metallic, meaning it can conduct jumpstart shocks. It can also serve to restrain mindflayers for the duration of the drill, making them unable to teleport, which is about the only use of it for high skill players.
