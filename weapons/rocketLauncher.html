The S.R.S which i will now write srs because i don't want to write all the dots all the time when refering to this weapon is the green variant of the rocket launcher. the left click is exactly the same as the blue variant just like with every other weapon in the game excluding the railguns because they want to be special. so what makes this weapon interesting the right click which fires out a litteral cannon ball. this ball is a non hitscan projectile which means it can be projectile booster like a shotgun blast. the ball will simple disintegrate when it hits a surface that isn't an ennmy and bounce upwards when it does actually hit an ennmy. this means that you can hit an ennmy with it, and projectile boost the ball afterwards dealing even more damage. note that the ball can be grappled towards you so if you are dumb enough to want to use this at longer ranges you can though when projectile boosted the ball will explode on any contact so be somewhat smart about it since it does have a somewhat long cooldown. there is a few tech bits you can do with it such as instakilling a cerberus by hitting it with the ball first then projectile boosting it into it again, another good use of this gun is its ability to stun insurectionists though in regular play it isn't all that interesting and actually comes in handy in the cybergrind as those are ennemies you want to prioritise or at least have out of your way to deal with other threats. there is also a tech called my balls explode where you fire the canon ball normally and shoot it with a hitscan weapon to make it detonate creating a short lingering explosion ball that can be quite handy to kill fodder ennemies like filth and obvioulsy hitting it with a baja blast makes a red explosion because it would be pretty lame otherwise.

The firestarter is the last of the weapons in the game with some semi interesting tech. left click is still the same cause why the hell would it change? anyways the right click will just spill oil on the floor that you can light on fire to deal damage to both yourself and ennemies. the oil will actually eliminate all friction from walking which means you can use it to preserve speed and have faster dashjumps because you don't loose momentum from touching the floor. this oil can also be applied to walls and ceilings but that isn't really useful. underwater however the oil will simply explode when you put it on fire. this oil can be applied to ennemies however which will just set them on fire, what else did you really expect from it? this does have special properties such as making oiled up maurices vulnerable to explosions which is the only way to achieve this. some ennemies will also actually take extra fire damage such as the flesh panopticon, the insurrectionist and of couse sisiphus prime, cause, well reasons.
