ULTRAKILL is a fast-paced ultraviolent retro FPS combining the skill-based style scoring from character action games with unadulterated carnage inspired by the best shooters of the '90s. Rip apart your foes with varied destructive weapons and shower in their blood to regain your health. its developpement was started by Arsi Patala who is kown online as hakita (see picture below) in 2020 where he published the first dev log of the game to his youtube channel under the name hakita.

He has stated it to be inspired by multiple old shooters like doom classic and arena shooters like quake, both games having had a huge impact on games as a whole but also being seen heavily in ULTRAKILL. All in the level design, the weapons, all reminiscent of the classic fps feel and even more noticeble in the graphics, with them being purposefully retro and low poly.
